Dataset

The 100 most-streamed songs on Spotify dataset was fetched from Wikipedia on August 1st, 2020.

I then created a playlist on Spotify with these songs, which I uploaded to Spotify's Organize your music app to extract each track's properties (tempo, duration, danceability, etc.).
